Louisiana, Maryland and Dallas shooting topic modelling

This is visualisation of VU Amsterdam summer school course "Big Data in Society" final project. Data about Dallas shooting (and shootings that were previous to it) was gathered from New York Times Twitter, Facebook page and articles. After data cleaning topic modelling was used.   • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • New York Times articles
